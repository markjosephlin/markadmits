Questions

Frequently asked.

Do you work with both undergrad and MBA applicants?

Yes — I work with both. Whether you're applying to top undergrad programs or M7 business schools, the core of what I help with is the same: building a compelling narrative that makes you impossible to ignore.

Do you guarantee admission?

No one can guarantee admission — and anyone who says otherwise is being dishonest. What I can guarantee is that your application will be the strongest possible version of you. Nothing left on the table.

How many students do you work with at once?

I keep my client list intentionally small so every student gets my full attention. Spots are limited each cycle — if you're interested, book a call sooner rather than later.

What if I'm just starting to think about applying?

That's actually the best time to reach out. The earlier we connect, the more we can shape your narrative and position your profile for the schools you're targeting. Book a free call and let's figure out where you stand.
